Personal Growth Hack

Accelerate your development in the next 6 months.

Three Areas to Hack:

Change your focus

Focus on optimization. The world needs us to be solutions oriented. Ask yourself "What project can I start working on that will change the world?"

Nourish your body

Eat a strict, clean diet. Eat healthy fats, organic vegetables and good-quality protein. Avoid sugar. Make sure to exercise and recharge.

Always be learning

Our world is changing so quickly. Developing new, unique skills is essential to succeeding. Always be up to date with the latest news in the industry of your passion.
